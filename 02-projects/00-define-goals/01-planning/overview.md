## What is Nexus?

Nexus is an **AI-powered work organization system** that remembers YOU across sessions.

### The Core Problem It Solves:

**Normal AI conversations are stateless**: Every chat starts from scratch. You re-explain your goals, your context, your preferences. Exhausting.

**Nexus is different**: Everything persists in Memory files. When you return tomorrow, Nexus loads YOUR goals, YOUR projects, YOUR patterns automatically. Like continuing a conversation with someone who remembers you.

### What Makes It Powerful:

🧠 **Persistent Memory** - Never start from scratch again
🎁 **Skills System** - Reusable workflows (you'll learn this in Project 02)
🎯 **AI Suggestions** - Collaborative intelligence based on YOUR context
📊 **Just-in-Time Loading** - Only loads what you need, when you need it

**Note**: You'll learn the full system gradually across 4 short sessions (35-40 min total)
