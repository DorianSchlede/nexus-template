## 4. Behavioral Signals (Kaufsignale)

| Signal | Indikator | Priorität |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| **Active Search** | Sucht nach AI Agent Lösungen | Hoch |
| **Pain Expression** | Beklagt Agent-Qualität | Hoch |
| **Budget Allocated** | AI/Automation Budget vorhanden | Hoch |
| **Trigger Event** | Neue Funding-Runde, Wachstum | Medium |
| **Competitor Usage** | Nutzt ähnliche Tools | Medium |

**Trigger Events to Watch**:
- New funding announcement
- Leadership changes (new CTO, VP Ops)
- Rapid hiring (AI/ML roles)
- Product launches requiring automation

## ICP Sentence Formula

**Template**: "[ROLE] at [COMPANY] who have [DIFFERENTIATORS] and need [SOLUTION]."

**Examples**:
- *Enterprise*: "CIOs at multinational retailers struggling with legacy POS who need cloud-native solutions."
- *Mid-market*: "Ops leaders at fintech scale-ups expanding into Europe who need compliance automation."

**Critical Note**: The sentence is output of rigorous analysis, not a shortcut. Generic statements without data validation are useless.
