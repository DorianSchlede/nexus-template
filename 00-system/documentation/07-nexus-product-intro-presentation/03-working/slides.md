### Key Talking Points

**Root Cause #1: Web AI = Stateless**
> "ChatGPT, Claude.ai - all stateless. Close the tab? Everything's gone. That's why knowledge gets lost in chats."

**Root Cause #2: No Organizing System**
> "Even with Claude Code, without a system you get 47 files named 'Final_v3.docx'. Where's your past work? Who knows."

**Root Cause #3: Knowledge Stays in Individuals**
> "Your top performer figured out the perfect process. But it's in their head. Team can't benefit. They leave? Knowledge gone."

**Root Cause #4: No Reusable Processes**
> "Client A: 12 weeks. Client B: Start over, another 12 weeks. No standards, no templates, no learning curve."

**The Solution**
> "You need three things: Memory (Claude Code), Structure (Nexus), and Reusability (Skills). That's what we're building today."

**Transition**:
> "So what does that structure look like? Let's see..."
