# Insights

Strategic synthesis and knowledge management.

## Structure

- `patterns/` - Recurring themes from research
- `decisions/` - Key decisions & rationale
- `meeting-prep/` - Meeting preparation materials
- `slack-extracts/` - Slack channel extractions
- `technical/` - Technical research & analysis
- `core-papers/` - Curated foundational papers
  - `_index.md` - Paper index with tiers
  - By topic: context-engineering, agent-memory, multi-agent, evaluation-safety

## Purpose

Synthesize learnings from:
- Interview analysis
- Experiment results
- Market research
- Technical exploration

Output: Actionable patterns and documented decisions.
