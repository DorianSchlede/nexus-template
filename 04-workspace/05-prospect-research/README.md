# Prospect Research

Lead research and targeting.

## Structure

- `target-companies/` - Company profiles & research
- `target-contacts/` - Individual contact research
- `lead-lists/` - Curated outreach lists

## Workflow

1. Research companies matching ICP in `target-companies/`
2. Identify key contacts in `target-contacts/`
3. Compile prioritized lists in `lead-lists/`
4. Hand off to `06-outbound/` for campaigns
