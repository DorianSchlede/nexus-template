## Hypothesis

**We believe** capped optimization tiers with transparent limits (Free: 10, Pro: 100, Team: 300) will result in **lower churn** (<5% monthly) compared to "unlimited" pricing, because customers understand exactly what they're paying for and don't feel surprised by usage-based charges.

**We'll know when** we measure:
- Monthly logo churn <5% (vs industry average 5-7%)
- Churn reasons: <10% cite "pricing confusion" or "unexpected costs"
- Customers rate pricing transparency 8+/10
- 60%+ of churned customers cite "not using enough" (not "too expensive")
